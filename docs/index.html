    🤔 What is Dledle?

    Dledle is a daily game composed of daily games. 🤯  There is a new, random selection of games to play every day!

    The goal is to beat at least 3️⃣ of the 5️⃣ randomly selected 'dle games.

    If you win before playing all five 'dles, you can optionally keep playing for ⭐️ bonus stars ⭐️! Share the 🔗link when you're done so your friends can see your Dledle score with all of your scores in each 'dle!

    You can ⏭️ skip any 'dle if you want, but you won't get a new one (skipping essentially counts as a loss).

    ℹ️ About Dledle

    Dledle is an open source project created as a joke / commentary on the absurd amount of daily 'dle games. But also it exists to reduce the wall of daily 'dle share text in your group chat. 🧹

    To report bugs, suggest features, or contribute to the project, check out the GitHub page!

    To support the project, tell people about it! 🤗
